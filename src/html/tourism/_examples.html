Traditional Music and Dance Performances

Our tours often include live performances of traditional music and dance, allowing visitors to experience the rich cultural heritage of the favelas. These performances provide an opportunity for cross-cultural exchange and appreciation.

Artisan Workshops

Visitors can participate in hands-on workshops with local artisans, learning traditional crafts such as weaving, pottery, and painting. These workshops offer a deeper understanding of the community's artistic traditions and the significance behind them.

Community Festivals

Depending on the time of year, visitors may have the chance to join in on community festivals and celebrations. These events are vibrant displays of local culture and provide a unique insight into the communal spirit and traditions of the favelas.
