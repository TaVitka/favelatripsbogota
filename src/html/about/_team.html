Meet
the Team

  • Community Liaison & Tour Guide

    Sofia Rodriguez

    Sofia Rodriguez joined Favela Trips Bogota as a tour guide and quickly became an integral part of the team. With a background in social work and community development, Sofia is dedicated to fostering positive relationships between visitors and residents. Her expertise ensures that the tours are not only educational but also supportive of local initiatives.

  • Co-Founder & Lead Tour Guide

    Alejandro Martinez

    Alejandro Martinez, one of the co-founders of Favela Trips Bogota, has over a decade of experience in the tourism industry. Born and raised in Bogota, Alejandro has an intimate knowledge of the city's favelas and a deep respect for their culture and history. His passion for storytelling and community engagement makes every tour a unique and enriching experience.

  • Operations Manager & Environmental Specialist

    Carlos Rivas

    Carlos Rivas oversees the day-to-day operations of Favela Trips Bogota, ensuring everything runs smoothly. With a degree in environmental science, Carlos is passionate about sustainability and eco-friendly tourism practices. He is committed to minimizing the environmental impact of tours while maximizing their educational value.
