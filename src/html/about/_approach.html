Our Approach

At Favela Trips Bogota, our approach to conducting favela tours is rooted in respect, authenticity, and positive impact. We believe in creating meaningful experiences that highlight the rich culture and history of Bogota's favelas while ensuring that our presence benefits the local communities.

  • Community Partnerships

    We have established partnerships with various community organizations within the favelas to support local initiatives. These partnerships ensure that a portion of our proceeds goes directly back into the community, funding projects such as educational programs and infrastructure improvements.

  • Educational Workshops

    Our tours often include educational workshops led by local experts, providing visitors with deeper insights into the culture and history of the favelas. These workshops cover topics such as traditional arts, local history, and social initiatives aimed at improving community well-being.

  • Supporting Local Businesses

    We collaborate with local artisans, food vendors, and other small businesses to create a truly immersive experience for our guests. By incorporating these businesses into our tours, we help to boost the local economy and showcase the unique talents and products of the favela residents.
