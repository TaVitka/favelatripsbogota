Our Story

Favela Trips Bogota was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate locals who wanted to share the hidden gems of their city with the world. They saw the potential to create meaningful connections between visitors and the vibrant communities within Bogota's favelas. Starting with just a few tours, the company quickly grew as word spread about the unique and authentic experiences they offered. Through dedication and a deep respect for the local culture, the founders built a company that prioritizes both education and sustainability. Today, Favela Trips Bogota is a trusted name in responsible tourism, known for its commitment to the community and enriching travel experiences.
