Maria Alvarez

«Growing up in the favela has taught me the value of community and resilience," says Maria Alvarez, a local artist. "Despite the challenges, we come together to support each other and create a vibrant, thriving neighborhood filled with art and culture.»

Jorge Hernandez

«As a youth mentor, I see firsthand the incredible potential of our young people," shares Jorge Hernandez. "They are full of creativity and ambition, and with the right support, they can achieve amazing things and contribute positively to our community.»

Lucia Ramirez

«Running a small business in the favela has its hurdles, but it also brings immense pride and satisfaction," explains Lucia Ramirez, a local market vendor. "The sense of community here is unparalleled, and we all work hard to uplift each other and make our neighborhood a better place.»
