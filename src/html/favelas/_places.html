• El Mirador Park

    El Mirador Park offers stunning panoramic views of Bogota, making it a popular spot for both locals and visitors. The park is also a community gathering place, hosting various cultural events and activities throughout the year.

  • La Casa de la Cultura

    La Casa de la Cultura is a vibrant cultural center that showcases the artistic talents of the favela residents. It features art exhibits, music and dance performances, and workshops that celebrate the rich cultural heritage of the community.

  • San Juan Market

    San Juan Market is a bustling hub of activity where you can find a variety of local produce, handmade crafts, and delicious street food. The market is a reflection of the favela's entrepreneurial spirit and offers a glimpse into the daily life of its residents.
