Safety and Logistics

  • Safety Measures

    We prioritize the safety of our visitors by partnering with local community leaders and providing trained guides who are familiar with the area. Our tours are designed to ensure a secure and enjoyable experience, with well-planned routes and regular safety briefings.

  • Respectful Behavior

    We encourage respectful behavior and interaction with residents by educating our guests on local customs and traditions. Visitors are advised to ask for permission before taking photos and to engage with locals in a courteous and considerate manner.

  • Practical Information

    Our tours offer convenient transportation options, including pick-up and drop-off services from central locations in Bogota. Tours typically last between 3 to 6 hours, and we recommend wearing comfortable attire suitable for walking and weather conditions.
